I maintain that, with the possible exception of math majors and philosophy majors (and maybe not even in those cases), undergraduates are better served by a serious course in inductive logic than they are by a course in deductive logic. (A real course in introductory inductive logic, that is, not a critical thinking course.) There are at least a few inductive logic textbooks that are suitable.
